Tikka Masala

Description

How to make a delicious Tikka Masala

What ingredients to use

Ingredients needed

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Marinade
  • Sauce
  • Naan Bread

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Fry the chicken
  3. Add the sauce
  4. Cook the rice
  5. Make naan bread from scratch
  6. Combine everything
  7. And TIKKA MASALA
