Italian Pizza

Description

How to make a delicous Neopolitan Pizza

What ingredients to use and what tools

Ingredients needed

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Combine Flour, water, salt and yeast
  2. Use a stand mixer to knead to a smooth dough has formed
  3. Let it rise
  4. Toss it out to a round pizza
  5. Add tomato sauce, cheese and toppings
  6. Put it in the oven
